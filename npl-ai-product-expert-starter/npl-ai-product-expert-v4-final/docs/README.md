# NPL AI Product Expert v3

This version adds a stronger expertise layer and two new modes:

- Lesson Delivery: turns a topic into a class flow with speaking script, interaction and exercises.
- Research Radar: separates anchored knowledge, emerging signals and weak signals for AI products, agents and financial services.

The knowledge base now includes:

- AI product foundations.
- Financial services AI adoption.
- AI agents for banking and insurance.
- Governance, risk and evaluation.
- Training architecture.
- Current AI signal radar 2026.
- Upcoming things and weak signals.
- Financial services AI product examples.
- Lesson delivery and voice interface direction.
- Source tiers and curated source register.

This is still a text-first prototype. The next meaningful product step is adding a voice rehearsal mode, not an avatar.
