# Interactive Voice Expert v1

## Recommended delivery path

1. Prepared delivery: use Source-Grounded Lesson Builder or Voice Delivery Prep to generate blocks.
2. Rehearsal: use ElevenLabs block audio for selected blocks only.
3. Interactive Q&A: use Interactive Voice Expert beta.
4. Webinar participant: later, only after moderation, disclosure, permissions and fallback controls are designed.

## First use case

The voice expert should answer financial-services AI product questions in 45-90 seconds:

- AI tools vs AI products.
- Bottom-up adoption vs product discipline.
- AI agents in banking and insurance.
- Governance, risk and evaluation.
- Evidence discipline and claims to verify.

## Operating principles

- Concise by default.
- Do not over-answer.
- Make uncertainty explicit.
- Do not invent examples.
- Say when claims need verification.
- Use NPL point of view, not generic AI commentary.
- In regulated contexts, be firm about ownership, controls, evidence, monitoring and human oversight.

## Current limitations

- Does not yet join Zoom/Teams as an attendee.
- Does not yet have full RAG/source retrieval inside realtime voice.
- Should not be used for unsupervised external delivery.
- Cost should be monitored.
