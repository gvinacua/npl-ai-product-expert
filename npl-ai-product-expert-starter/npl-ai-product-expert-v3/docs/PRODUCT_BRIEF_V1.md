# Product Brief v1 — NPL AI Product Expert

## Working name
NPL AI Product Expert

## Product definition
An AI expert speaker and trainer created for Net Positive Labs to support corporate training programs, talks, panels and executive briefings about designing, building and managing AI products.

## Initial market
Financial services: banking, insurance, fintech, payments, wealth, risk and corporate transformation.

## Core use case
The agent appears as a guest speaker inside an AI training program and can deliver or prepare several sessions about how to design, build, test and manage products with AI.

## Initial modes
1. Guest Speaker
2. Training Designer
3. Panel / Q&A Expert
4. AI Sparring Partner

## Differentiation
The agent is not an AI generalist. It is focused on the productization of AI: how organizations move from tool adoption and isolated pilots to AI-enabled products, workflows and operating models.

## Core thesis
The next advantage in AI will not come from access to models or tools alone. It will come from the ability to convert AI into trusted, governed and measurable products and workflows.

## Build strategy
Start with a deployable internal app, not a Custom GPT. Use GitHub + Vercel + OpenAI API. Add knowledge base and voice progressively.
