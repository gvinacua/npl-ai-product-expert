# Research Knowledge v1

This build includes a first curated knowledge base generated from current external research and official/consulting sources around AI products, enterprise AI, agents, governance, banking and insurance.

The knowledge base is intentionally compact. It is not meant to be an exhaustive library. It gives the agent a sharper point of view and a better vocabulary for financial-services AI product training.

## Included knowledge files
- 01_ai_product_foundations.md
- 02_financial_services_ai_adoption.md
- 03_ai_agents_for_banking_insurance.md
- 04_governance_risk_evaluation.md
- 05_training_program_architecture.md
- 06_source_register.md

## Important limitation
This is a static research pack. The app does not browse the web live. For client-facing claims, recent numbers, regulation or named-company examples should be verified before use.
